Wolf Texture Manifest
=====================

Format:
- map_char: ASCII symbol used in map layout
- code: short codename for the object/tile
- asset: texture asset name; BMP file is textures/<asset>.bmp
- notes: source or special handling

Other Map Symbols
-----------------

map_char=>  code=spawn_east    asset=-               notes=player spawn/orientation
map_char=<  code=spawn_west    asset=-               notes=player spawn/orientation
map_char=^  code=spawn_north   asset=-               notes=player spawn/orientation
map_char=v  code=spawn_south   asset=-               notes=player spawn/orientation
